About SHALTORO

Your trusted partner in premium furniture solutions since 2008

Our Journey

Founded in 2008 in Kanpur, SHALTORO has grown to become one of the leading suppliers of high-quality furniture across various sectors, including office, institutional, bank, hospital, and home furnishings.

Our Philosophy

We are committed to delivering innovative and ergonomic designs, offering greater choice, environmentally-friendly solutions, and exceptional customer service, all while ensuring value for money.

Our Reach

With showrooms and workshops across Kanpur and nearby regions, our experienced team continually innovates, adapting to global trends and technological advancements.

Why Choose SHALTORO?

  • Over 10 years of experience in modular office and bespoke furniture.
  • Equipped with high-tech manufacturing and post-forming machinery.
  • Offering customized solutions for kitchens, wardrobes, and living spaces.
  • Registered GEM portal supplier for government departments.
